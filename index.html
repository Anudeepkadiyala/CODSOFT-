    Starboy ft.Daft Punk

    "Starboy" is a song by the Canadian singer-songwriter
    the Weeknd featuring the French electronic duo Daft Punk.
    It was released on September 21, 2016, through
    XO and Republic Records,as the lead single from his
    third studio album of the same name (2016).
    The artists wrote and produced the song with Doc McKinney and
    Henry "Cirkut" Walter, with Jason "DaHeala" Quenneville
    receiving additional credits.
    It is an R&B and electropop song that
    reflects themes of extravagance and celebrity life.
